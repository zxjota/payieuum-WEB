1. Introduction

Welcome to Malagboters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Effective Date" of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

Malagboters is designed as an offline-first application, with a focus on local data processing and privacy protection. Most features work without an internet connection, and your personal style preferences remain on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the application includes:

Personal Preferences

Color preferences, style choices, and fashion selections that you voluntarily save within the application to enhance your user experience.

Local History Data

Your color coordination history, saved outfits, and favorite combinations are stored locally on your device. This data is only accessible on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Device Information

Technical information about your device, such as your device model, operating system version, and app usage statistics to help us improve app performance.

Color Selection Data

The colors you select and the combinations you create are processed locally on your device using our color harmony algorithms. This data remains private and is not shared unless you explicitly choose to export it.

3. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the application to:

  • Generate personalized color coordination suggestions based on your selected primary color.
  • Process color harmony algorithms (complementary, analogous, triadic, monochromatic) locally on your device.
  • Save and organize your color coordination history for future reference.
  • Enable search, filtering, and management of your saved color combinations.
  • Improve the application's color suggestion algorithms based on anonymous, aggregated usage data (only with your consent).
  • Support dark/light mode preferences based on your device settings.
  • Provide an optimized user experience for your specific device.

4. Data Storage & Local Processing

Malagboters is designed with privacy and offline functionality as core principles:

Offline Operation

All application resources, including color algorithms, style databases, and processing logic are completely localized. The application functions fully without an internet connection.

Local Storage

Your data including color history, favorites, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

On-Device Processing

All color analysis, harmony calculations, and suggestion generation occur directly on your device. No personal color preferences or style choices are transmitted to external servers.

Data Management

You have full control over your data with features including search, pagination, pull-to-refresh, swipe-to-delete, and one-click clearing of your entire history.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information. Your data remains on your device. However, we may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Legal Compliance

If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable law, rule, or regulation.

Anonymous Analytics

With your explicit consent, we may collect completely anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help us improve the application. This data cannot be traced back to you personally.

Service Providers

We may share non-personal, aggregated data with third-party service providers who assist us in application analytics and improvement, but only in anonymized form that cannot identify you.

6. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your information. Since Malagboters operates primarily offline with data stored locally on your device:

  • Your color preferences and style data are protected by your device's native security mechanisms
  • No personal style data is transmitted over the internet unless you explicitly choose to share it
  • All color processing occurs locally on your device
  • We implement industry-standard security practices in our codebase

While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the information within the application, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We encourage you to use device-level security features to further protect your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data within Malagboters:

  • Access & Export: View and export your color history and preferences at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual items with swipe-to-delete or clear all data with one-click
  • Search & Organization: Search through your history and organize with pagination
  • Theme Control: The app automatically adapts to your device's light/dark theme preference
  • Opt-Out: Choose not to share anonymous analytics (if this feature is enabled)

Since your data is stored locally, you maintain physical control over it through your device. To completely remove all data, you can clear the app data through your device settings or use the one-click clear function within the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Malagboters is a fashion and color coordination tool that can be enjoyed by users of all ages with an interest in personal style, but we encourage parents to supervise their children's use of the application.

We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

For significant changes that affect how we handle your data, we will provide a more prominent notice within the application itself. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
